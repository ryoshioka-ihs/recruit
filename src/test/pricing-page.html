Some of Your Questions

Do you work mostly on-site or remote?

We assign you with three experts to ensure your technology is always working hard on your behalf. Part of your Effective Care Team includes an On-Site IT Manager. He or she is responsible for blueprinting your system and continuing to collaborate, with you, as your company grows (as well as maintaining or upgrading your physical network.) After all, technology is moving mind-numbingly fast. It’s our job to guarantee your company is always on the leading edge of technological leveraging.

What industries do you specialize in?

We’ve worked with a wide range of companies throughout the USA and Europe. The industries we’ve worked include (but certainly aren’t limited to): Legal Services, Media & Entertainment, Investment Banking, E-Commerce, Postal Services, Non-Profit, Publishing, State & Local Government, Advertising, Branding & Marketing, Medical etc.

How can I utilize your Business IT Solutions?

We've created comprehensive packages that fulfill all of your Business IT needs. And depending on the size of your company, you can choose the package that has the amount of Help Desk, On-Site Management hours, On-Cloud Data Storage and expert consultation as you want... or your budget allows.

Will I benefit from professional Business IT?

The real answer is... Every business benefits from receiving our IT Solutions. If you want to compete, especially while we're still in the midst of a sluggish economy, your company requires the best possible foundation to become the undisputed leader in your marketplace.
